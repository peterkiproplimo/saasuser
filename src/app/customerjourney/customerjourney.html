Stage Customer Journey Objective
Awareness
  • Sees ads, referrals, social media mentions
  • Searches for SaaS billing solutions
  • Reads blog articles or watches videos
  • Create initial brand recognition
  • Drive traffic to landing pages
  • Capture interest with targeted content
Interest
  • Visits landing page or demo request page
  • Reads feature list, pricing, case studies
  • Engages with chat or fills contact form
  • Educate and qualify leads
  • Answer basic product questions
  • Encourage sign-up or trial start
Sign-Up
  • Registers an account or submits form
  • Verifies email or phone number
  • Receives welcome email or call
  • Capture lead officially into the system
  • Begin customer relationship
  • Reduce signup friction with support
Onboarding
  • Fills organization profile
  • Invites team members
  • Completes setup wizard
  • Guide customer to key features
  • Enable a successful first use
  • Lay foundation for retention
Activation
  • Uses product features (e.g., sends invoice)
  • Engages with core value actions
  • Completes profile/data import
  • Deliver first product value
  • Trigger “aha” moment
  • Increase chances of paid conversion
Billing Setup
  • Selects plan (monthly, annual, etc.)
  • Adds payment method (card, mobile money)
  • Reviews or downloads invoices
  • Convert to paying customer
  • Ensure billing is seamless & compliant
  • Start revenue generation
Usage
  • Uses dashboard and tools daily
  • Monitors subscriptions or invoices
  • Collaborates with teammates
  • Drive engagement and stickiness
  • Track behavior for future upsells
  • Keep customer actively using features
Support
  • Submits support ticket or chats live
  • Reads help docs or community answers
  • Attends webinars or requests help
  • Improve customer satisfaction
  • Reduce churn risks
  • Resolve friction quickly
Expansion
  • Adds new team members
  • Upgrades to higher-tier plan
  • Buys add-ons or integrations
  • Grow account revenue
  • Enhance feature adoption
  • Reward power users
Renewal / Churn
  • Gets renewal notifications
  • Renews or cancels plan
  • May provide feedback
  • Retain users through value & support
  • Understand reasons for churn
  • Attempt win-back if needed
